Compute trigonometric ratios (angles are in radians and degrees)
godarda@gd:~$ octave
...
octave> pi
ans = 3.1416

octave> deg2rad([0, 90, 180, 270, 360])
ans =

        0   1.5708   3.1416   4.7124   6.2832


octave> rad2deg([0, pi/2, pi, 3/2*pi, 2*pi])
ans =

     0    90   180   270   360
