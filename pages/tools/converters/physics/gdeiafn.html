The Energy Converter is a powerful tool designed to facilitate instant conversions between various units of energy. Whether for physics, engineering, or nutritional calculations, it provides accurate results across a wide range of energy measurements.

How to Use
  • Input Value: Enter the energy value you wish to convert in the input field.
  • Select Unit: Choose the starting unit (e.g., Joule, Kilocalorie) from the dropdown next to the input.
  • Customize Results: Use the "Select Conversion" dropdown to choose which target units to display.
Supported Units

This tool supports conversion between the following energy units:

  • Metric / SI: Joule (J), Kilojoule (kJ), Watt-hour (Wh), Kilowatt-hour (kWh)
  • Thermal / Nutritional: Gram Calorie (cal), Kilocalorie (kcal), British Thermal Unit (BTU), US Therm (thm)
  • Physics / Mechanical: Electronvolt (eV), Foot-pound (ft⋅lb)
