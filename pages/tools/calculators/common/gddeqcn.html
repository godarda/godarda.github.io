How to use:

  • Input: Type or paste a number (or text containing digits) into the box.
  • Analysis: The tool extracts all digits and calculates their sum and average.
  • Trim: Use the Trim button or Ctrl+Enter to remove leading/trailing whitespace.

Example: For 10 digits like 1234567890:

  • Sum: 1+2+3+4+5+6+7+8+9+0 = 45
  • Average: 45 / 10 = 4.5
