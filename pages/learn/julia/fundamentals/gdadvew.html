Exponents
# e raised to the power of 0
julia> exp(0)
1.0

# exp(0) - 1
julia> expm1(0)
0.0

# e raised to the power of 1
julia> exp(1)
2.718281828459045

# exp(1) - 1
julia> expm1(1)
1.7182818284590453

# e raised to the power of 2
julia> exp(2)
7.38905609893065

# exp(2) - 1
julia> expm1(2)
6.38905609893065
Logarithms
# natural logarithm (base e)
julia> log(0)
-Inf

# natural logarithm (base e)
julia> log(2)
0.6931471805599453

# base-10 logarithm
julia> log10(0)
-Inf

# base-10 logarithm
julia> log10(2)
0.3010299956639812

# base-2 logarithm
julia> log2(0)
-Inf

# base-2 logarithm
julia> log2(2)
1.0
Square root and Cube root functions
# square root
julia> sqrt(100)
10.0

# cube root
julia> cbrt(27)
3.0

# 2 raised to the power of 10
julia> 2^10
1024
