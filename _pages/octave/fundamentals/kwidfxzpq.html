Arithmetic operations on complex numbers
octave> complex(4+5j)+complex(2+6j)
ans =   6 + 11i
    
octave> c1=4+5j
c1 =  4 + 5i

octave> c2=2+6j
c2 =  2 + 6i

octave> c1+c2
ans =   6 + 11i

octave> c1-c2
ans =  2 - 1i

octave> c1*c2
ans = -22 + 34i

octave> c1/c2
ans =  0.9500 - 0.3500i
